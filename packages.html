Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book? +
We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for peak wedding season (November-February and May-July) to secure your dates.
Do you travel for outstation weddings? +
Yes! We travel pan-India and globally. Travel and accommodation expenses are to be borne by the client and are separate from package pricing.
Are the products organic? +
Absolutely. We use 100% natural, farm-sourced Rajasthani henna mixed with essential oils (Nilgiri & Lavender) and lemon sugar. No chemical dyes or PPD.
How long does the stain last? +
With proper aftercare, a dark rich stain can last between 7-14 days depending on your skin type and exposure to water.
